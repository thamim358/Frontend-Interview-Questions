💡 Certification Strategy for React Frontend Engineers

Don't collect certs — collect capabilities. Prioritize 1 Cloud (AWS Developer) + 1 Framework (Meta React) + 1 Specialty (Accessibility or CI/CD). That combination proves you can build, deploy, and maintain production-grade React applications. Free certs (Meta Front-End audit, Next.js workshops) are great for resume keywords; paid certs (AWS Developer, CPACC) move the salary needle.
